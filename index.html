01

The problem I kept seeing on the ground

"Every case I managed revealed the same underlying issue: the back-office HR infrastructure was incomplete, outdated, or simply missing."

Having worked as an HR Ops specialist within EOR environments, primarily on the French market with additional support across EMEA, I noticed the same pattern, repeatedly.

Not because teams lacked expertise. But because managing front-office delivery, responding to clients and employees in real time, leaves no bandwidth to build what should have been there from the start.

Add to this the complexity of French labour law, its constant legislative changes, and the difficulty of applying them correctly in a tripartite EOR relationship. The result: avoidable escalations, repeated questions, and processes that aren't documented or standardised.

That's exactly what I work on. And why Moyana HR Studio exists.

What I do — and what I don't

Case management frameworks and decision trees
I don't do

Ticket or case processing

Payroll management
Recruitment

I work in the back-office — so your HR Ops team can focus on what they do best.
